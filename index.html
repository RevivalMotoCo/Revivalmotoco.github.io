Who We Are

A Bloke
With a
Workshop

My name's Conrad. I've had a lifelong obsession with motorcycles — from BSAs and Moto Guzzis to Aprilias and KTMs — and a working knowledge of how to keep most of them running. Mildly competent, but genuinely passionate.

Revival Moto Co is straightforward: I find motorcycles that have been neglected, ignored, or left to quietly decompose, and I make them right. All kinds — anything with a bit of character and a story worth continuing.

No fakery. No shortcuts. Every bike gets properly looked at before it goes anywhere near a for-sale ad. If I find something worth knowing about, you'll know about it.

Based in Shropshire. Working out of a proper workshop. Making it up as I go along, but in a mildly competent way.

How It Works

Find It.
Sort It. Sell It.

01

Find

Barn finds, private sellers, long-term storage, the odd garage that hasn't been opened since 1987. No auction fodder. No Cat N hiding behind a confident asking price and a fresh tin of Hammerite.

02

Sort

Every bike gets looked at properly. We fix what needs fixing, note what doesn't, and don't try to make it into something it isn't. What you see is what it is.

03

Sell

You get the full picture. What we found. What we did. What we left alone and why. Buy with your eyes open or don't buy at all — we'd genuinely rather you didn't if it's not right for you.
